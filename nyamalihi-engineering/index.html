Who We Are

Kenya's Trusted Civil Engineering Partner

Founded in 2010, Nyamalihi Engineering Ltd is a fully registered civil engineering firm delivering roads, buildings, bridges, and infrastructure projects across Kenya.

Our team of NCA-registered engineers combines technical rigor with a deep understanding of Kenya's terrain, regulations, and communities. We have completed over 120 projects — from rural access roads in Meru to commercial buildings in Nairobi's CBD.

We work with county governments, national agencies, private developers, and NGOs. Every project is managed with clear timelines, transparent budgets, and quality-first workmanship.
